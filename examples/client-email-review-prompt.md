# Example: Client Email Review

```text
Review this client update email with revüe before I send it. Make sure it explains what changed, why it changed, what the client should look at, and anything that still needs a decision.
```

Expected behavior:

- Use `client-delivery` or `implementation-review` mode depending on the content.
- Confirm the recipient-facing message is clear.
- Flag unsupported claims.
- Identify missing links or attachments.
- Recommend send / revise / block.
